Mission Statement
Plants can be more difficult for some and they may come naturally to others. Either way, we're here to help! We're here for those plants you've put so much effort into and aren't seeing it grow to where you'd like it to be and those plants you just have no idea what to do with anymore.
Plants can be scary at first, and that's okay! Let us show you how to tame them.


About us as a company
From the ripe age of 19, the founder, Jonathan Torres, had an affinity for plants. Starting with a ZZ plant that was gifted to him and he slowly grew his plant collection little by little. However, as he grew his collection he ran into problems: plants weren't responding well after months of trial and error of trying to get the plants to grow, little knowledge on how to take care of certain plants, and even running into trouble with repotting a plant. He went through the struggle so hopefully you don't have to.
Now, we're here to help you grow your plant collection to the place you'd like it to be.
